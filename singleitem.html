Malabar Biriyani

10-15 Mins

Malabar biryani is rich with a liberal use of ghee and aromatic with a medley of spices like cinnamon, cloves, cardamom and nutmeg, to name a few. Surprisingly, this biryani is not mouth-numbing “hot” but spicy in a good way. It does not include red chilies, either whole or in powder form.
